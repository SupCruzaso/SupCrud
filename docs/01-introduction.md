# 🚀 Introduction to SupCrud

**SupCrud** is an innovative **SaaS (Software as a Service)** platform designed to revolutionize customer support through intelligent ticket management (PQRS).

Built by **Team Crudzaso**, our mission is to bridge the gap between complex enterprise support systems and small-to-medium businesses by providing a seamless, AI-powered integration.

### ✨ Key Features

- **Multi-tenant Workspace:** Each company manages its own environment with a unique `workspace_key`.
- **Hybrid Data Engine:** High-consistency relational data in **PostgreSQL** combined with high-scale flexible ticket storage in **MongoDB**.
- **Plug & Play Widget:** A lightweight JavaScript snippet that transforms any website into a support hub in seconds.
- **AI Integration:** Smart analysis of tickets to prioritize and categorize customer needs (Coming soon).
