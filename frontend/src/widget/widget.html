SupCrud Widget Preview

This is the final customer interface. The widget in the bottom-right corner allows your users to submit PQRS (Tickets) and track them in real-time powered by our AI engine.

Production Installation

Copy this script before the closing </body> tag of any website: 

<script
  id="supcrud-script"
  src="https://cdn.supcrud.com/widget.js"
  data-workspace="YOUR_WORKSPACE_ID"
  data-api="http://localhost:3000/api/v1"
  data-color="#4f46e5"
></script>
